This mod replaces all the music in the game with various Sonic the Hedgehog tracks! Mostly from SA1, but a decent chunk of SA2 and some others sprinkled in as well. (Plus some fun non-Sonic surprise tracks!) 
As a heads up, I chose -most- of the songs to fit the mood of the song it's replacing, not specifically trying to tie character themes to other character themes. (I.E. Amy's theme not being for Kairi, it simply doesn't fit well.)
As a general note for the series, some songs did not change either due to personal preference or because the game literally doesn't have a spot for it for whatever reason. (But it's like 2 or so, or they're hard baked into pre-rendered cutscenes.)
